Dimensions: The Lore Wars

Dimensions: The Lore Wars is an AR-enhanced, collectible card game with real-time combat. In this game, players will immerse themselves in the lore of different worlds, unlocking special cards and battling in various environments to collect powerful abilities. The game uses AR technology to bring the game world to life, allowing players to interact with the world around them. Collectible cards are used in real-time combat, where players can unlock new cards and abilities as they progress through different levels and battles.

Game Features:

  • - Real-time battles with collectible cards
  • - Immersive AR experience bringing the game world to life
  • - Unlockable abilities and powers
  • - Developed using Unity for mobile and AR platforms.
  • - Various levels and environments to explore
  • - AR integration for physical interaction with the game world

Pollutant

Pollutant is a 2D pixel-style survival game developed in Unity, inspired by games like Vampire Survivors. In this game, players must survive waves of environmental enemies and toxic creatures in a polluted world. With each wave, the enemies grow stronger, and players must collect power-ups, upgrade weapons, and restore balance to the environment. Fight against the toxic elements, survive as long as possible, and cleanse the world!

Game Features:

  • - Survival gameplay against endless waves of enemies
  • - 2D pixel art style with smooth animations
  • - Fight against environmental enemies, toxic creatures, and pollution-based monsters
  • - Collect power-ups and upgrades for weapons and abilities
  • - Dynamic waves of enemies that grow progressively harder
  • - Restore the environment and heal by collecting health boosts
  • - Explore multiple levels with unique challenges

Vanguard AR Experience

This augmented reality project allows users to scan a Vanguard card, which triggers an interactive AR experience. Once scanned, the card "pops out" in the real world and activates multiple dynamic actions, providing an engaging experience for users. The AR system uses advanced image recognition and tracking to deliver a variety of visual effects and actions. This was made as a proposal for Bushiroad Expo

AR Features:

  • - Scan Vanguard card to trigger AR experience
  • - Interactive actions such as animation, sound effects, and 3D objects
  • - Uses ARCore (Android) or ARKit (iOS) for smooth tracking
  • - Provides immersive user interaction with physical and virtual worlds
  • - Developed using Unity with AR Foundation for cross-platform compatibility

AR Collectible Card System

A custom-designed multi-rarity collectible card system developed for Dimensions: The Lore Wars. These cards are more than visuals — they can be scanned via QR/AR to unlock in-game skills, stat boosts, and ultimate abilities. Designed with a modular system, the cards can also be traded, expanded, and integrated into future titles.

System Features:

  • - Multi-rarity card design (Common, Rare, Legendary, Ultimate)
  • - AR scanning unlocks skills directly in-game
  • - Supports stat boosts, ultimate skills, and modular expansion
  • - Potential for trading and collection mechanics
  • - Designed in Photoshop & integrated with Unity + ARCore
