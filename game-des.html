Pollutant - Core Gameplay

Pollutant is a survival-environmental strategy game where pollution spreads dynamically across the world. Players must clean, protect, and rebuild while facing challenges such as resource scarcity, toxic hazards, and the ethical choice between industrial growth and ecological balance.

Core Gameplay

The core gameplay of Dimensions: The Lore Wars revolves around AR-enhanced combat, mission-based progression, and skill acquisition through card scanning. This loop ensures variety and replayability.

×

Storyline

The story follows VRanger as he journeys across fractured dimensions to fight the LoreBreakers. Each battle restores balance to corrupted storylines, blending narrative immersion with gameplay.

Map Flow

Battle maps are designed to be modular arenas. Each map features strategic obstacles, pathways, and enemy spawn points to enhance both challenge and pacing during encounters.

Battle Gameplay

Battle gameplay combines combo-based combat, AR-acquired ultimate skills, and enemy squad coordination. Encounters are paced to balance intensity and strategy.
